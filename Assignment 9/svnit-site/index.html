About SVNIT

Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT), Surat, is one of India's premier engineering institutions and an Institute of National Importance. Established in 1961, SVNIT was formerly known as Sardar Vallabhbhai Regional College of Engineering & Technology (SVRCET) before being upgraded to an NIT in 2002. The institute is centrally funded by the Government of India and is renowned for its excellence in engineering, technology, and research.

Academics

SVNIT offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs across multiple disciplines.

Admissions

Admissions are conducted through JEE (B.Tech), GATE (M.Tech), and Institute-level exams for Ph.D. programs.
